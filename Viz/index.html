 Interface Instructions:
 
 MAIN 
 1. Read the data creation instructions, and fill in the text areas accordingly.
 2. Hover over the quality panel to understand each parameter of quality.
 3. Parameter colors:
       - Red : Bad : Sample Modification Recommended
       - Orange : Mediocre : Sample Modification at User Discretion
       - Green : Good : Sample Modification Not Required
 4. The accept/reject field indicates the probability that your sample will be accepted if 
    submitted as is without modification.
 5. The pie chart shows the percentage of samples accepted and rejected on hover.
 6. The  number of samples that have not yet been reviewed for acceptance, by the
    administrator are shown in the pending review box 

 MORE DETAILS 
 1. The line chart indicates the quality of each sample you have submitted over time.
 2. Hovering on the points of the line chart shows the parameter-wise colored stats of the
    sample, and whether it's been accepted or rejected.
 3. Clicking on the points of the line chart pulls up the respective sample.
 4. The rank list shows the ranks of all crowd source workers, in terms of
    the percentage of good samples created.



 Data Creation Instructions:

 1. View the: 
       PREMISE: Generated caption from system
    and enter alternate captions as follows:
       HYPOTHESIS A: Alternate caption from user that is definitely true.
       HYPOTHESIS B: Alternate caption from user that may or may not be true.
       HYPOTHESIS C: Alternate caption from user that is definitely not true.
 2. Click the Review button to check sample quality (you can review the sample prior to
    final submission as many times as you want).
 3. You can submit the sample at any time by clicking on the Submit button.
 4. The New Premise button generates a new premise.
 5. Click on the Clear button to clear all fields.
 6. The Autofix button auto-modifies the sample.
 7. Click on the Undo button to reverse the last auto-fix operation.
